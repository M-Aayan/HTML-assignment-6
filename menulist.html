Qorma

Qorma is a traditional Indian dish made from mixed vegetables and rice. It is often served with dal or rice, and sometimes accompanied by chutney.


Biryani

Biryani is a traditional rice dish consisting of lentils, mixed vegetables, and a variety of spices, often served with a curry sauce or garam masala.


Kabab

Kabab is a popular dish made from fried chicken, beef, or lamb, served with rice, lentils, and other vegetables, often with a sauce made from tomato, cumin, and garlic.


Karahi

Chicken karahi, or kadai chicken, is a chicken dish from South Asia. It is known as gosht karahi when prepared with goat or lamb meat instead of chicken.
